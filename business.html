The website we have created is based off Business located in New York City's five boroughs. With the data provided to us from the NYC Open database we were able to retrieve all the information we needed from every business ever made in the city of New York. We found just displaying the information from inputs was to simple. We have incorprated a Booststrap dropdown within our code, so when you press on a certain borough it diplays cards. On these cards we have given key information that may be useful to people that want to know the business.
